\subsection*{CClearTag: Clear the Tag Bit}

\subsubsection*{Format}

CClearTag cd, cb

\begin{center}
\cheritwoop[header]{0xb}{cd}{cb}
\end{center}

\subsubsection*{Description}

Capability register \emph{cd} is replaced with the contents of \emph{cb}, with
the tag bit cleared.
